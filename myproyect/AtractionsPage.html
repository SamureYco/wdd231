Major Attractions in Chiclayo

Túcume Pyramids

Explore the ancient Túcume Pyramids, a remarkable archaeological site consisting of 26 pyramids built by the Lambayeque culture.

Royal Tombs of Sipán Museum

Visit the Royal Tombs of Sipán Museum, which houses incredible treasures and artifacts from the Moche civilization.

Chiclayo Market

Experience the vibrant atmosphere of the Chiclayo Market, a bustling hub where you can find fresh produce and delicious street food.

Santa Maria Cathedral

This beautiful cathedral is a historical landmark and a stunning example of colonial architecture.

Paseo Las Musas

A lovely park ideal for leisurely strolls, featuring sculptures and a serene environment.

Sipán Archaeological Site

Visit the site where the Lord of Sipán was buried, known for its rich archaeological significance.

Chiclayo Beach

Relax at the beautiful beaches near Chiclayo, perfect for sunbathing and enjoying fresh seafood.
